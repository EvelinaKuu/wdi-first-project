Learn nouns

Welcome to Learn nouns! This game is about learning nouns in a different language. You will see a sentence with a highlighted word, please translate that word by choosing one of the three given options. Take your time and don't worry if you pick the wrong word, just try again, this is all about learning the words. If you want to start from the beginning just click on the start again button in the end of the page. There are 10 levels of each language. Please choose a language below.

Feeling confident? Click on random and get a combination of sentences in all the languages.
